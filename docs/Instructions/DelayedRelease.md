---
title: Why is there a delay in releasing tokens
---



Due to ExinOne's [business model](. /TradingMethod.md), when a token generates a large number of transactions in a short period of time, there may be a shortage of tokens in ExinOne to pay you, so we will need to top up the token from the exchange to Mixin by on-chain transfer, and then the token will be released to you. The release of coins will be delayed due to the time required for on-chain transfer.

The transfer of assets is normal and your assets are very safe. When you confirm the transaction, the order will be filled in real-time. Only the crediting time to your account will be delayed appropriately.



If you encounter a delayed coin release, the system will prompt you for the maximum number of tokens that will not be delayed. Suppose you want to trade the relevant tokens, but don't want to wait too long. If you can accept to buy more times, you can try to designate the receiving account as the trading account and then buy the number of tokens that will not be delayed, you can do this operation repeatedly until you buy the number of tokens you want.
